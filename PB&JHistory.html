Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches have a long and storied history in the United States.Peanut butter was first invented in the late 19th century, and quickly became a popular food in America due to its affordability and versatility. Jelly, on the other hand, has a longer history, with references to fruit preserves-like spreads dating back to ancient civilizations. The combination of peanut butter and jelly as a sandwich filling first became popular in the early 20th century, and has since become a staple of American cuisine.
