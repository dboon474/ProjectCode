About Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwiches

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich, also known as a PB&J, is a classic sandwich made by spreading peanut butter and jelly on bread, very self explanatory. It is a popular anytime and anywhere.

Our Menu

We offer a wide variety of peanut butter and jelly sandwiches, including traditional versions as well as creative twists on the classic. Here are a few of our most popular options:

  • Classic PB&J: Made with creamy peanut butter and grape jelly on white bread.
  • PB&J with Banana: Made with peanut butter, jelly, and a sliced banana.
  • Toasted PB&J: Just like the classic, but toasted
